The Perfect Homemade Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe features a soft, chewy crust topped with a rich, garlicky tomato sauce and melted mozzarella. Customize with your favorite toppings like pepperoni, veggies, or sausage. With simple ingredients and easy steps, you'll have a delicious, fresh pizza in no time—perfect for any pizza night!

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
    • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F / 45°C)
    • 3 ½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the sauce:
    • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • inch of sugar (optional)
  • For the toppings:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (or more to taste)
    • Any toppings you like: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, sausage, etc.
    • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough:
    1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. In a large bowl, mix 3 ½ cups of flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Stir until the dough begins to come together.
    3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes, adding more flour as needed, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
    4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    2. Add tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning. Add a pinch of sugar if the sauce is too acidic.
    3. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Assemble the pizza:
    1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
    2. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two portions (if making two pizzas). Roll each portion into a round shape on a floured surface. Make the dough as thick or thin as you prefer.
    3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or baking sheet (with parchment paper if needed). Spread a thin layer of sauce on the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings on top of the cheese.

  4. Bake the pizza:
    1. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the hot pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven.
    2. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    3. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, slice, and enjoy!

Tip: For a crispier crust, bake the pizza on the lower rack of the oven, or pre-bake the crust for 5 minutes before adding toppings.
